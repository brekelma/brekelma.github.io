I'm a fifth year PhD student at USC working with Greg Ver Steeg and Aram Galstyan, using information theory for unsupervised machine learning.
In my PhD, I've worked on (invariant, disentangled) representation learning using rate-distortion (R-D) and the information bottleneck (IB) method.
More recently, I've been exploring information geometry, annealed importance sampling, and convex duality, along reinforcement learning on internship at DeepMind.
