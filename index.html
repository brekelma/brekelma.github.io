I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Vector Institute in Toronto, working with Alireza Makhzani. I graduated with my PhD from University of Southern California in May'22, working with Greg Ver Steeg and Aram Galstyan.

One common theme of my research is thinking about "Paths through Distribution Space", either for MCMC annealing paths between densities, for diffusion models, or more recently, for dynamical optimal transport. I tend to see convex dualities and Bregman divergences everywhere, and am also inspired by information geometry and information theory. In our recent !Mutual Information estimation paper, we also provide insights on InfoNCE contrastive losses used in self-supervised learning.

Finally, I've thought about regularized reinforcement learning on internship at DeepMind and (invariant, disentangled) representation learning with the information bottleneck method earlier in my PhD.
